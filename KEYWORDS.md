## üéØ Usage Instructions

### For Manual Testing:
1. Copy any keyword above
2. Paste into the search bar at `http://localhost:3000`
3. Press Enter or click Search
4. The appropriate industry view will load

### For URL Testing:
```
http://localhost:3000/?query=order%20pizza
http://localhost:3000/?query=blockchain%20development
http://localhost:3000/?query=buy%20shirts
```

### For ChatGPT Integration:
Feed these prompts directly into ChatGPT when the IndustryConnect AI app is installed. ChatGPT will route users to the correct industry view based on detected intent.
